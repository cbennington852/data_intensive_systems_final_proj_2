Important Health Insurance Terms

Deductible

The fixed amount you pay out-of-pocket before your insurance coverage begins to contribute.

Coinsurance

Your share of costs (e.g., 20%) for a service after you've already met your deductible.

Monthly Rate

The amount of money you pay each month for that specific healthcare plan.

Common Coverage Sections

Dental

Covers routine cleanings and more intensive procedures like fillings or oral surgery.

Vision

Focuses on annual eye exams and discounts or allowances for glasses and contacts.

Mental Health

Provides coverage for therapy, psychiatric evaluations, and substance abuse treatments.
